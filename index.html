Welcome to Trax

Trax Bar & Cafe, your first, last and possible only stop to or from Wellington

If you are going to an event at Westpac Stadium or simply looking for somewhere with a good vibe, come and enjoy a drink and a light meal at Trax Bar & Café. We have a large selection of Café food, freshly made daily on the premise, light meals and snacks. Piece of Cake Bakery satisfies the sweet craving with homemade cakes slices and takeaway coffee.

Be your own D.J. with Orange Door Music. Simply download the app and you have access to 20,000 songs.

We are available for private functions and group booking. Email us for special rates and further information.

Come and see us at Trax. “Its where people meet”
